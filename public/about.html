About Me

My name is Donna. I am from Mandaue City, Cebu. I graduated with a Bachelors Degree in Secondary Education Major in English. I am a licensed professional teacher and a freelancer.

I have a variety of hobbies and interests. I enjoy reading books, playing sports like badminton and volleyball, and exploring new places through travel. Additionally, I have a passion for photography and love capturing moments and landscapes.

I have been working in the teaching industry for the past five years. I have experience in roles such as ESL tutor, mentor, and administrator where I gained valuable skills in teaching, writing memos, creating monthly reports, organizing records, problem-solving, and communicating effectively to clients. I am now excited to take on new challenges and grow in my career.

One of my strengths is my ability to adapt quickly to new situations and learn new skills. I am also a good communicator and enjoy collaborating with others. As for weaknesses, I sometimes tend to be a perfectionist, which can lead to spending more time on tasks than necessary. However, I am actively working on finding a balance and prioritizing tasks effectively.

In the future, I aspire to be a Web Developer, such as taking on a leadership role as a developer or starting my own business. I am also committed to continuous learning and professional development to stay updated with industry trends and advancements. Ultimately, I aim to make a positive impact in my chosen field and contribute to the success of my organization.
